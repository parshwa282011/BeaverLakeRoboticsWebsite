Beaver Lake Midde School Robotics

Programs
FTC

FTC stands for FIRST Tech Challenge in which we build our robots from metal peices and some 3d printed parts to try to make our robot score the most amount of points in a match with another team as a alince against two other teams

FLL

FLL stands for FIRST Lego Leauge in which we make robots out of legos and a few techinical peices. In this we have to make a Inovation project which is trying to solve a problem in the world.
