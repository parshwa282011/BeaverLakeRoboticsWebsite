Beaver Lake Midde School Robotics

Donations

Our club relies primarily on donations from generous individuals to sustain its operations. We organize outreach events to collect funds for purchasing parts and covering other expenses. Additionally, we host a significant fundraiser that serves as our primary source of income, ensuring the continuous functioning of the club.

About Us

Our club consists of three FLL teams and two FTC teams. The FLL teams are designated for 6th and 7th graders, while the FTC teams are tailored for 7th and 8th graders.
