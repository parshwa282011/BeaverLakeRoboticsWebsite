Beaver Lake Robotics Club

About Us

Our club consists of three FLL teams and two FTC teams. The FLL teams are designated for 6th and 7th graders, while the FTC teams are tailored for 7th and 8th graders.


Our FTC Teams

Alpha Intelligence and Beta Bionix



Alpha Intelligence and Beta Bionix are sister teams that both work together to build and code the best robot that they can.


This year both teams participated Spencer League Meets and Tesla Interleague Tournament where both teams were selected for playoffs. In WTI both teams were picked to do playoffs and Alpha Intelligence won.


Outreach

This year, Alpha Intelligence and Beta Bionix collaborated extensively on outreach initiatives. We mentored three school FLL teams, showcased our robot to kids during Salmon Days, and conducted a Girl Scout presentation to teach them about FIRST.



Our FLL Teams

Robotic Dogs, Alpha Tech and Spark Bots



Robotic Dogs, Alpha Tech, and Spark Bots are sister teams collaborating closely to construct and program the most exceptional robot possible.


This year, all the teams competed in a tournament aiming to progress to the next level of competition. Only one team, Robotic Dogs, managed to advance. This marked the first time an FLL team from our club advanced to the state level. At the state competition, Robotic Dogs secured the 11th position.
