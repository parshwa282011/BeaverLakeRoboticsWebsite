Alpha Intelligence


Alpha Intelligence is a First Tech Challenge (FTC) team from Beaver Lake Middle School (BLMS). Our mission is to build the best robot, running the most efficient software that competes amongst the best teams in FTC every year.


This year we participated in the Spencer League Meet and Tesla Interleague Tournament where we won the Control Award (2nd Place) and were the first pick of the Semi-Finalist Alliance. In the Washington Tech Invitational (WTI), we were the second pick of the Winning Alliance.


Outreach
Alpha Intelligence was heavily involved in outreach efforts this year. We worked with Pakistan to establish an FLL team there, participated in community service activities, and partnered with Troop 571 to prepare over 2,600 sandwiches for Union Gospel's homeless shelter.
