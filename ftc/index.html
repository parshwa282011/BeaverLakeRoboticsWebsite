FTC stands for FIRST Tech Challenge in which we build our robots from metal peices and some 3D printed parts to try to make our robot score the most amount of points in a match with another team as a alince against two other teams

Alpha Intelligence and Beta Bionix are sister teams that both work together to build and code the best robot that they can.


In 2023-2024 season, both teams participated Spencer League Meets and Tesla Interleague Tournament where both teams were selected for playoffs. In WTI both teams were picked to do playoffs and Alpha Intelligence won.
